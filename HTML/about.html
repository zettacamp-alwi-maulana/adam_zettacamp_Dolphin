Welcome to MusikMu

Your Gateway to Indonesian Indie Music

Company Overview

MusikMu is a pioneering Indonesian music streaming platform that stands as a celebration of the diverse and vibrant world of Indonesian indie music. Born out of a deep passion for music and a commitment to supporting local talents, MusikMu offers an unparalleled collection of indie songs that connect music enthusiasts with talented local artists.

Service Description

At MusikMu, we are dedicated to fostering a sense of pride in Indonesia's rich musical heritage. Our user-friendly platform presents a seamless online music streaming experience, enabling users to discover, enjoy, and support a wide range of indie music from across the nation.

Plans of Service

MusikMu offers two distinct plans of service: "Free" and "Premium." Each plan comes with its own set of features to cater to the preferences of different users.

Free Plan

The "Free" plan on MusikMu provides users with access to a curated selection of indie songs, playlists, and albums. While using the "Free" plan, users can:

  • Listen to a limited number of songs per day.
  • Explore handpicked playlists and discover new artists.
  • Enjoy occasional ads between songs.
  • Create and customize personal playlists.
  • Access basic music recommendation features.
  • Participate in community discussions and forums.
  • Engage with artists through comments and likes.
  • Access to occasional live streaming sessions with artists.
    • Get insights into the creative process of artists.
    • Ask questions and interact with artists in real-time.
    • Experience intimate performances and behind-the-scenes moments.

The "Free" plan is perfect for users who want to dip their toes into the world of Indonesian indie music and explore the platform's offerings without any financial commitment.

Premium Plan

The "Premium" plan on MusikMu offers a more enhanced and ad-free music streaming experience. Subscribers of the "Premium" plan can enjoy the following benefits:

  • Unlimited skips and uninterrupted playback.
  • Access to the entire music library, including exclusive releases.
  • Offline downloads for listening without an internet connection.
  • High-quality audio streaming for an immersive listening experience.
  • Early access to new releases and special content.
  • Personalized music recommendations based on listening habits.
  • Exclusive invitations to virtual concerts and events.
  • Premium customer support for a seamless experience.
  • Direct interaction with featured artists.
    • Join private Q&A sessions with your favorite artists.
    • Receive exclusive content and updates directly from artists.
    • Participate in exclusive fan clubs and contests.

The "Premium" plan is designed for serious music enthusiasts who want to fully immerse themselves in the world of Indonesian indie music, support local artists, and enjoy a seamless and premium listening experience.

Company Behind MusikMu

MusikMu was founded in 2019 by a group of passionate music lovers and tech enthusiasts in Indonesia. Our mission is to empower local artists, showcase the depth of Indonesian indie music, and provide a platform where listeners can connect with the heart and soul of their local music scene.
