About This Game

Dive into a captivating clash of flora and undead as you step into the extraordinary world of "Plants vs. Zombies Heroes." In this epic battle for Neighborville, the age-old struggle between plants and zombies has evolved into an all-out card game showdown.


Build your ultimate team of heroic plants and mischievous zombies, each armed with their unique powers and abilities. Assemble your deck, strategize your moves, and engage in thrilling, turn-based battles across a variety of whimsical and treacherous landscapes.

Unleash the power of your favorite characters, from classic PvZ favorites to new, dynamic heroes. Harness the energy of sunflowers, launch explosive peas, summon undead hordes, and deploy a range of formidable tactics to outwit your opponents.


Embark on an adventurous journey through the vibrant streets of Neighborville, uncovering new cards, battling powerful foes, and mastering your deck-building skills. Engage in thrilling single-player campaigns or challenge friends and players worldwide in intense multiplayer battles.


With a captivating blend of strategy, humor, and heart, "Plants vs. Zombies Heroes" offers an enchanting gaming experience that's fun for all ages. Will you stand with the resilient plants or join the mischievous zombies in a quest for supremacy?
